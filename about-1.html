Code Queen is a UK registered charity that focuses on empowering young women through education and training that increases their employability. Last year we launched Code Queen in Kampala which is an all-female bootcamp designed to train young women in coding and soft skills. Code Queen has successfully run 4 cohorts and graduated 83 students since the launch in 2019.

Our Vision

To provide young girls with the support and information they need in order to make smart business & life decisions, and reach the pinnacle of success.

Our Mission

To empower girls & young women in East Africa through education & training.


SONAL KADCHHA, FOUNDER & CHAIR

British born with Kenyan-Indian roots, Sonal read Chemical Engineering at Queens’ College Cambridge, where she graduated with a First Class with honors. She set up ETC in 2008 after visiting the Masai Mara and noticed the lack of resources. Sonal is part of the “Extra Mile 40”, a list of 40 people in finance, recognised by the eFinancial News, who go further for good causes. Sonal was also a finalist for Clarins “Woman of the year” 2017 which recognizes the UK’s most visionary charity pioneers
